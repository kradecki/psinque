If you have a device running iOS, you're in luck since iOS devices have built-in native CardDAV support. If you will follow these simple steps, you will be running Psinque on your iPhone or iPad in no time.

Before you proceed, make sure that you have enabled CardDAV synchronization in your settings and have your unique login/password combination ready.

  1. Open the Setting menu.
  2. Select "Mail, Contacts, Calendars"
  3. Select "Add Account"
  4. Choose "Other"
