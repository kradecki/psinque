  1. How do you pronounce Psinqe?

    As the word "sync". The 'p' is silent as in "pshrimp".

  2. What is Psinque?

    To put it simply, it is the last phone book you will ever need. Psinque keeps contact details in your smartphone always up to date and makes sure that once you change your data, your friends' devices will be seamlessly updated. No harrasing pop-ups, no action required. Just pure magic.

  3. Kinda sounds like another social network

    Eat a Snickers! When you're hungry you are turning into a diva! But seriously, we are far from it. Our goal is only to materialize the connections from your real life in a digital world and make them persistant. If you feel like posting pictures of your food and checking in whenever you are in a toilet, use the services that were designed for it.

  4. How does Psinque work?

    Think of Psinque as a system of buckets interconnected by lines. You can put anything you want in your bucket or remove any numbers of items, but the connecting line will not be severed.

    Psinque does exactly the same with your contact information. Once a link (or as we call it - 'psinque') has been established, you can modify your phone numbers, change your name, modify or add a new address and we will automatically and seamlessly provide this data to your contacts.

  5. OK, so how much does it cost?

    Psinque is free of charge and we would like to keep it this way. But since great products require extrodinary people to keep them running, we would appreciate any form of financial support.

  6. Which devices are supported?

    Any modern device which supports CardDAV protocol (iPhone, iPad, Android phones) will work fine with Psinque.

  7. OK, so the idea is great. How do I configure my device?

    Depending on your device visit the iPhone, iPad or Android configuration guide.

  9. But I don't want people to find me here. This is just for friends!

    We know and we value your privacy! That's why if you do not have any public permits, only people who know your phone number or email address will be able to find you. And even than, all they will se is a message that such a person exist. Your data will still be hidden at this point. You will have to confirm that request in order to make your data visible for them.

  10. Who controls my data?

    You! In order to be able to use our service, we will have to store your data on our servers. But you decide who can access it and what you share with your contact. And should you decide to quit our service, your personal information will be completly delted from our servers as if it never existed.

  11. Can I influence who gets what kind of information?

    YES! Using Permits you can create different views of your profile - one for your family, one for your work buddies and one for your BFFs. Every person you establish contact with will be assigned one of those permits and thus will see only what you allow them to see.

  12. What is a Public permit?

    Imagine you have a company and would like to provide your contact data to many clients as you can. At the same time the idea of having thousands of contacts in your phone just doesn't seem right. Well, this is the reason, why we created public permits. It is basically a one way connection, where people feed on the data you provide at the same time retaining their privacy.

  13. Is there an app for my mobile device?

    We are planning to develop an application to support less tech-savvy users. But the best part is - you really do not need one. Configuring your device to run with psinqe won't take longer than 5 minutes.
