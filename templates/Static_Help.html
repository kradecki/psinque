Editing your Profile

If you decide that Psinque is just the right thing for you, the first thing you should do is edit your personal information in the profile page. It should be as complete as possible. Try not to omit any information as the more you include, the more flexible with creating your personas you will be. This section will guide you through the process and highlight the most relevant information. Remember - we value your privacy! To find out how will the information provided by you used, review our Terms of Service and Privacy policy.

Creating your Personas

Once you have set up your profile it is time to create and adjust your personas. Think of personas as of digital disguises - with their assistance you can create multiple views of your profile and decide what will your contacts see. You could create one persona for your work colleagues (and hide your private contact data from them), one for your best friends and one for your family.

Managing your Psinques

Ok, so you have completed your profile and created first personas. Now it's time to create your first connections, or as we refer to them - psinques. This chapter will guide you through the process of managing incoming and outgoing psinques.

Configuring your mobile devices

Only once you synchronize your mobile device with Psinque, will you be able to discover and harness the true power behind it. Psinque uses CardDAV protocol so synchronize your phonbeook and most of the devices on the market either support it natively or with an assistance of a third party application.
