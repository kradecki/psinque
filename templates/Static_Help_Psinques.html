What is a 'psinque'?

Psinque is our name for a connection between two contacts. However unlike real life connection, a psinque will exist forever, unless consciously severed. In real life, if a peson moves, changes phone number, email address or gets married, chances are that a contact might be lost.

Psinques can divided into incomming, outgoing or both. Let's discuss them in detail and see how they impact the contents of your mobile device's phonebook.
