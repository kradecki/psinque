What is Psinque?

Psinque is the last phone book you will ever need. But it is a different kind of phonebook, one that you'll immediately fall in love with. Psinque keeps contact details in your smartphone up-to-date at all times and makes sure that once you change your own data, your friends' devices will be automatically and seamlessly updated. No harrasing pop-ups, no action required. Just pure magic.

How to pronounce Psinque?

As the word "sync". The 'p' is silent as in "pshrimp".

Is Psinque another social network?

Psinque is far from it. Our goal is only to materialize the connections from your real life in a digital world and make them last forever. If you feel like posting pictures of your food and checking-in whenever you are in a toilet, use the services that were designed for it.

Our mission

Psinque is a young and dynamic team of people commited to one mission: to help you stay in touch with your contacts indefinitely with as little overhead and engagement as possible.

Our story

The idea behind Psinque was born in 2012. Krzysztof, a native Pole living in Germany had to purchase a new prepaid SIM card each time he visited his home country. His friends complained about him having constantly change his numbers and he himself often forgot to inform everyone about the switch. Together with Stanisław they came up with a solution to the problem and started working on back then a no-named tool that evolved into Psinque. Piotr joined the team a few months later to support us in legal matters.
