Concept behind Psinque

Psinque is the last phone book you will ever need. Think of it as of a set of your name cards that you give to your friends. Except that once you give a name card, you will be able update it later so that your friends will always have up-to-date information. All that updating will be handled by Psinque so you just update your data and let us do the rest.

Back-end Technology

Psiqnue uses the CardDAV protocol to keep your phonebook up to date. CardDAV is a relatively new technology, but has already gained wide acceptance and is supported either natively (e.g., iOS, GMail, Zimbra, ownCloud) or with the assistance of third party applications (e.g., CardDAV-sync for Android).
